How Fraud Guard Works
🔍
Detection

Monitors for large single transfers (>50% balance), velocity spikes (>5 txns/hour), and transfers to unknown addresses.

⏳
Slow Transfer

Instead of instant drain, funds move in 3 stages of 30%, 40%, 30% with a mandatory 24-hour wait between each stage.

✅
Cancel Window

If it was a false alarm, the real owner can cancel at any time before a stage executes. ETH stays safe — nothing is lost.
