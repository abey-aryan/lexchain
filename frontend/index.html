Workflow

Designed around the exact legal lifecycle contracts enforce.

Every step in LexChain mirrors a real legal process — but replaces the human intermediary with cryptographic enforcement.

📜
Step 1
Owner creates the legal record
Write a will, create an escrow deal, or notarize a document. Each action writes an immutable transaction to the blockchain — witnessed by the entire Ethereum network.
🤝
Step 2
Parties confirm conditions are met
For escrow, both buyer and seller confirm. For a will, the 180-day clock runs automatically. For notarization, anyone can verify instantly with just the file.
⚡
Step 3
Smart contract executes automatically
Funds transfer, records seal, audit trail closes. No lawyer signs off. No company processes the payment. The code runs exactly as written — no exceptions, no delays.

Audit Trail

Every action. Permanent. Public.

Any government authority, court, or auditor can call getFullAuditLog() directly on the AuditTrail contract. There is no company to subpoena. There is no server to hack. The record exists as long as Ethereum exists.

🔒
Append-only architecture
Entries can never be edited or deleted. The log only grows. Even the contract deployer cannot modify past records.
🔍
Hash integrity verification
Every entry includes a keccak256 hash of its data. Anyone can call verifyEntryIntegrity() to prove a specific record was never tampered with.
📤
One-click JSON export
Download the complete audit log as a structured JSON file for legal documentation, court submission, or tax reporting.
